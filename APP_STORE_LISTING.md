## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Stop guessing. Start profiting.**

Margin is the ultimate profit analysis tool for resellers who source at thrift stores, yard sales, estate sales, and flea markets. Simply scan any item with your camera and get instant buy/pass recommendations based on real sold listings.

**HOW IT WORKS**

1. Point your camera at any item
2. Our AI identifies what it is
3. See the expected sale price based on recent sales
4. Get a clear FLIP or SKIP verdict
5. Know exactly what to pay to hit your profit target

**WHAT YOU CAN SCAN**

• Watches - Rolex, Omega, Seiko, Tag Heuer, and more
• Trading Cards - Pokemon, Sports Cards, Magic: The Gathering
• Sneakers - Nike, Jordan, Yeezy, New Balance
• Collectibles - Funko Pop, LEGO, Hot Wheels, Action Figures
• Electronics - Gaming consoles, cameras, audio equipment
• And more with Open Market search

**REAL DATA, REAL PROFITS**

Unlike other apps that guess prices, Margin pulls from actual sold listings. We show you what items ACTUALLY sell for - not wishful thinking prices.

• See median sale prices from recent transactions
• Account for all fees (platform fees, shipping, costs)
• Get accurate profit calculations
• Know your maximum buy price instantly

**FEATURES**

✓ AI-Powered Scanning - Point, shoot, profit
✓ Flip/Skip Verdicts - Clear recommendations you can trust
✓ Profit Calculator - See net profit after all fees
✓ Inventory Tracking - Keep track of items you've bought
✓ Scan History - Review all your past analyses
✓ Profit Goals - Set daily targets and track progress
✓ Offline Mode - Works even without internet
✓ Dark Mode - Easy on the eyes during long sourcing trips

**SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS**

FREE:
• 5 scans per day
• 7-day scan history
• 25 inventory items

PRO ($24.99/month):
• Unlimited scans
• Permanent history
• Batch scanning mode
• Yard Sale swipe mode
• AR Profit Overlay
• PSA cert verification

• Tax export (Schedule C)
• Multi-platform listing tools
• Sales analytics

**MADE BY RESELLERS, FOR RESELLERS**

We built Margin because we needed it ourselves. No more spreadsheets. No more guessing. No more buying items that sit in your garage for months.

Download Margin and turn your next sourcing trip into a profitable adventure.
